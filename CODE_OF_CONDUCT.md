# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in this project and community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive community include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Respecting the sanctity of the Quran in all discussions

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassing, attacking, or insulting comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Disrespectful comments about religious beliefs or practices
- Attempting to modify or misrepresent Quranic text
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Threats or violent language

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to:
- Remove comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct
- Ban any contributor whose behavior is harmful or disrespectful

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Issue trackers and pull request comments
- Community forums and communication channels
- Any other spaces managed by the project

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Email**: Open an issue or email the project maintainers
2. **Include details**: Description, context, and any evidence
3. **Confidentiality**: Your report will be handled confidentially
